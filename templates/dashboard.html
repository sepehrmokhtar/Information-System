Guidelines for Using the EHR Application

This guide provides instructions for navigating and using the key features of the EHR system, including Add Patient, Patient List, Doctor Profile, and related functionalities.

1. Add Patient Page

The Add Patient page is where doctors can add new patients to the system. Follow these steps:

  • Doctor Details: Enter the doctor's email in the "Doctor E-mail" field. Use the calendar icon to set the patient's admission date.
  • Patient Basic Information: Fill in the fields for the patient's information.
  • Demographic Information: Select the patient's gender and provide the patient's age.
  • Vital Signs: Enter the patient's core temperature and heart rate.
  • Immunization Status: Check applicable vaccines.
  • Medical History: Add details about disease history and family history.
  • Laboratory Test Results: Fill in lab values like WBC, RBC, HCO3, Glucose.
  • Allergies and Habits: Enter allergy and habit information.
  • Medication History: Add details about current and past medications.
  • Review of Systems (ROS): Provide a comprehensive summary of the review of systems.
  • History of Present Illness: Include details like chief complaint and SOAP notes.

2. Patient List Page

The Patient List page displays all registered patients. It allows you to manage patient records effectively:

  • View Patient Profile: Click on a patient's name to view their full profile.
  • Edit Patient Information: Modify patient details by clicking the Edit button.
  • Delete Patient Record: Remove a patient record using the Delete button.

3. Doctor Profile Page

The Doctor Profile page allows doctors to manage their information:

  • View Profile: Review your details on the profile page.
  • Edit Information: Update your information like name, email, contact, and specialization.
  • Save Changes: Click Save to update your profile.

Tips for Effective Use:

  • Ensure Accuracy: Double-check information, especially medical details.
  • Handle Deletes Carefully: Deleting records is permanent.
  • Maintain Updates: Keep your profile updated to ensure accurate contact details.
